## Equipment Needed
- Micro USB socket
- 2 B&F metal screws (Fastener Supply PMSSS 256 0025 PH)

![](P1080977.jpg)

## Assembly

1. Position the micro USB socket in its slot on the side of the box opposite the power switch. Put the screws in, and tighten.
