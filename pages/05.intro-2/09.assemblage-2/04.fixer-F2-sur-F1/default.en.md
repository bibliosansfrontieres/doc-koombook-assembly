## Equipment needed
* Deck F1
* Deck F2
* 4 metal button head screws (M 3x5)

![](_MG_5228.JPG)

## Assembly
1. Place the deck F2 above the deck F1
![](_MG_5229.JPG)   
2. Fasten the 4 corners of F2 deck with the help of the metal screws
